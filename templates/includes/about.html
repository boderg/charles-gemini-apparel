About

Charles Gemini Apparel

Charles Gemini Apparel is an independent, small business based in London.
We primarily sell t-shirts and hooded sweatshirts with custom in house designs.
All our designs are printed on premium quality fabric and are available in various sizes and colours.
Please view our size guide for an indication of our sizes and their fit.
Feel free to contact us via the contact form.

Thank you for visiting our site and we hope you find something you like.
Please check back regularly for new designs and products.
We are always looking to improve our products and services so any feedback is welcome.
We hope you enjoy your shopping experience with us.
